## How BFS is basically your Social Life 🌐

Ever noticed how news spreads? You tell your 3 best friends. Then they tell their friends. You don’t tell your best friend’s cousin’s roommate before you tell your own brother!

That’s **Breadth-First Search (BFS).**

📍 **The Vibe:** It explores level by level. It’s "wide" before it’s "deep."

### 🛠️ How it works (The Queue 🚶‍♂️🚶‍♀️):

1. Start at the root (You).
2. Visit all your immediate neighbors (Level 1).
3. Only then move to their neighbors (Level 2).

✅ **Use Case:** Finding the shortest path in an unweighted graph (like "People You May Know" on LinkedIn).

**Remember:** BFS = The Ripple Effect 🌊
